Blepharoplasty

Blepharoplasty (BLEF-uh-roe-plas-tee) includes surgery to repair droopy eyelids that may involve removing excess... skin, muscle and fat.Removing excess tissue from your upper eyelids, lower eyelids or both can improve vision and make your eyes appear younger and more alert.

Botox Injections

Botox injections are the best known of a group of medications that use various forms of botulinum toxin to... temporarily paralyze muscle activity. This toxin is produced by the microbe that causes botulism, a type of food poisoning.

Dermabrasion

Dermabrasion is a skin-resurfacing procedure that uses a rapidly rotating device to sand the outer layers of skin. After dermabrasion,... the skin that grows back is usually smoother and younger looking.Dermabrasion can be done alone or in combination with other cosmetic procedures.

Liposuction

Liposuction is a surgical procedure that uses a suction technique to remove fat from specific areas of the body, such as from... the abdomen, hips, thighs, buttocks, arms or neck. Liposuction also shapes (contours) these areas. Other names for liposuction include lipoplasty and body contouring.

Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty (RIE-no-plas-tee), also referred to as nose surgery, is a surgery that changes the shape of the nose.... The motivation for rhinoplasty may be to change the appearance of the nose, its function or both.Rhinoplasty can modify bone, cartilage, skin or all three.
